1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Contact us: Name, email address, and message content when you use our contact form
  • Subscribe to our newsletter: Email address only
  • Use interactive features: Your wildlife checklist selections (stored locally in your browser, not on our servers)

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • General geographic location (country/region level only)

We do not collect precise location data or create detailed user profiles.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide wildlife travel advice
  • Send newsletter content you've subscribed to
  • Improve our website content and user experience
  • Analyze aggregate traffic patterns (not individual behavior)
  • Detect and prevent technical issues or abuse

We Never: Sell your personal information to third parties, share your email with tour operators without your consent, or use your data for targeted advertising.

3. Cookies and Tracking

Essential Cookies

We use essential cookies that are necessary for the website to function properly, including:

  • Session cookies to maintain your browsing session
  • Preference cookies to remember your settings

Analytics

We use privacy-focused analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Our analytics:

  • Do not track individual users across websites
  • Do not use cookies for tracking purposes
  • Collect only aggregate, anonymized data
  • Are compliant with GDPR and CCPA

Local Storage

Our Wildlife Checklist feature uses your browser's local storage to save your checked species. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to our servers
  • Can be cleared by you at any time using the "Reset Progress" button

4. Data Sharing

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Email service providers who help us send newsletters (bound by confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • With your consent: When you explicitly ask us to share information with a specific party

We do not share personal information with advertisers, data brokers, or tour operators.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure email handling practices
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security reviews and updates

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and protect your own data.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Unsubscribe: Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Data portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object: Object to processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@wildlifevacation.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is designed to be family-friendly and educational. However:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • Children should use our site with parental supervision
  • Parents may contact us to review or delete any information collected from their child

If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. International Transfers

Our website is hosted in the United States. If you access our site from outside the US, your information may be transferred to and processed in the US. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers, including:

  • Standard contractual clauses approved by relevant authorities
  • Privacy Shield certification where applicable
  • Compliance with GDPR for European visitors

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page with an updated date
  • Sending an email to subscribers for material changes

We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@wildlifevacation.com
  • Contact Form: wildlifevacation.com/contact

We will respond to privacy-related inquiries within 30 days.

Questions? We're happy to explain any part of this policy in plain language. Just contact us and we'll help clarify.
