Common Questions

Contact FAQs

How quickly will I receive a response? +

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days (Monday-Friday). For urgent safari planning needs, please indicate this in your message and we'll prioritize your request. During peak travel planning seasons (January-March and September-November), response times may be slightly longer.

Can you help plan a custom wildlife trip? +

While we don't book trips directly, we provide personalized recommendations for destinations, ethical operators, and experiences based on your family's interests, budget, and travel dates. Our advice is completely free and unbiased‚Äîwe don't receive commissions from operators we recommend. We'll point you toward verified ethical operators who can handle the actual booking.

Do you offer consulting services for travel agencies? +

Yes, we offer consulting services for travel agencies and tour operators looking to improve their wildlife tourism offerings. This includes ethical certification guidance, staff training, destination development, and marketing consultation. Contact our partnerships team at partnerships@wildlifevacation.com for more information about our B2B services and pricing.

I'm a conservation organization‚Äîhow can we collaborate? +

We love partnering with conservation organizations! We offer content partnerships, data sharing agreements, and promotional support for conservation initiatives. If you're a wildlife reserve, sanctuary, or conservation NGO, please reach out to partnerships@wildlifevacation.com with details about your organization and how you'd like to collaborate.

Can I report an unethical wildlife tourism operator? +

Absolutely. We take ethical concerns very seriously. If you've witnessed or experienced unethical wildlife tourism practices, please use the contact form and select "Ethical Tourism Questions" as the inquiry type. Provide as much detail as possible including operator name, location, date, and what you observed. All reports are kept confidential and help us maintain our ethical standards database.
