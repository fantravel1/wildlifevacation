Fascinating Giraffe Facts

ğŸ¦’ Incredible Neck

A giraffe's neck can be over 6 feet long, yet contains only 7 vertebrae - the same number as humans! Each vertebra is just much, much larger (about 10 inches each).

â¤ï¸ Powerful Heart

A giraffe's heart weighs about 25 pounds and pumps blood at twice the pressure of other mammals to reach their brain. Special valves prevent blood from rushing to their heads when they bend down.

ğŸ‘… Blue Tongue

Giraffes have dark blue-purple tongues up to 20 inches long! The dark color may protect against sunburn while they spend hours eating acacia leaves in the hot African sun.

ğŸ”‡ Silent Giants

Scientists once thought giraffes were mute, but they actually communicate using infrasound - frequencies too low for humans to hear. They also hum to each other at night!

ğŸ¦µ Born to Run

Baby giraffes are about 6 feet tall at birth and can stand within 30 minutes! They can run with the herd within just 10 hours of being born.

ğŸ¨ Unique Patterns

Like human fingerprints, no two giraffes have the same spot pattern. Researchers use these patterns to identify and track individual giraffes in the wild.
