Yellow fever vaccination is required. Malaria prophylaxis recommended for some areas. Consult a travel doctor 4-6 weeks before your trip.

Long sleeves, long pants, and DEET repellent are essential. Mosquito nets at lodges provide nighttime protection. It's the tropics - expect insects!

ğŸ  Choose Good Lodges

Reputable eco-lodges have trained naturalist guides, proper meals, and screened accommodations. Quality varies widely - research thoroughly.

Quick-dry clothing, waterproof bags for electronics, and rubber boots (often provided). Nothing ever fully dries in the rainforest!

Dry season (May-Oct) has easier trails and more wildlife at rivers. Wet season (Nov-Apr) offers canoe access to flooded forest - different but magical.
